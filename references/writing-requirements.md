# CAPUarticle Writing Requirements

Source: `C:\FIRST-CC\学校推文\新材料学院宣传部新闻稿撰写要求.docx`.

## Word Document Format

- Title: 黑体二号/小二, centered.
- Text under title, images, and review line: 宋体五号, centered. Review line: `审核 王阳合`.
- Body: 宋体四号, first-line indent 2 characters, 1.5 line spacing.
- Images: center-align images, place each image at the corresponding article position.
- Image captions: place below each image, 宋体五号, centered, concise and descriptive.

## Writing Rules

- News releases should use a concise 总-分-总 structure where possible. Keep to three paragraphs when possible and no more than four paragraphs for concise news briefs.
- Push-article style drafts may be longer and more flexible, but should still be formal, accurate, and suitable for school publication.
- Leadership titles must use standardized descriptions. Acceptable patterns include:
  - 学院党组织负责人
  - 教学工作负责人
  - 团学指导老师
  - 学生会指导老师
  - 新材料学院团委负责人
- Do not use `新材料学院团委书记`.
- If a leadership title is unclear, ask the event organizer/user immediately and record the answer. Do not guess.
- Avoid sensitive wording and political-position problems. The article must not include wording that violates national policies or laws.
- Party meeting names, policy documents, and national policy terms must use official public terminology exactly. Example: `十五五规划`.
- Do not freely modify official terms, including punctuation, when the term has an official form.
- Do not include religion-related wording.
- Every news release must include an accurate date. Do not use vague time expressions such as `近日`.
- Date format in the article should be `2026年 月 日` style with the actual month and day filled in.

## Submission Requirements

- Submit the Word document plus original images as a compressed package.
- Word document and compressed package naming format: `【YYYYMMDD】文章标题名称`, where the date is the event date.
- Example: `【20260521】以学铸魂强信念 笃行实干担使命——新材料学院组织发展对象线上参加2026年第1期党员发展对象培训班结业典礼`
- Original images used in the document should be named in article order: `图1`, `图2`, `图3`, etc.
